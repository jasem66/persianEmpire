Nasir al-Mulk mosque also called pink mosque and mosque of colors was built along with a hammam, a school and a reservoir during the Qajar era in Shiraz by the order of Mirza Hasan Ali Nasir al-Mulk. The construction of this complex took almost 11 years (1876_1887). This mosque and its beautiful combination of tiling and brick working is marvelous and attracts anyone's attention.
